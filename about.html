Proper Parking Company's

Our business model is unique and easily contrasted to other parking service companies. By way of example, but not by way of limitation, the following may demonstrate to you the manner in which we attempt to set ourselves apart:

1. We maintain an in-house legal department that handles any and all disputes that may arise with patrons at no cost to our clients. In addition, all city license and permits; insurance; bonding and third party agreements relating to servicing our clients are prepared in-house at no cost to the client.

2. We maintain an in-house accounting department that includes a licensed certified public accountant that prepares financial reports on a daily basis

3. Our on site supervisors have earned college degrees or are in the process of completing their college degrees.

4. Our on site parking staff is instructed that first impressions are paramount and that being curdious, friendly, being well spoken, well kept and being attentive is our "motto" and we have site inspections without notice to assure that our image is maintained.

5. We emphasize a "no waiting policy" for patrons requiring promptness and attentiveness by the on site staff.

6. We perform detailed background investigations assuring clean driving records, insurability and obtaining recommendations from prior employers.
